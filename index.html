About the Event

QEVRA Fest’26 Is A Premier Technical Event Organized By The Department Of Computer Science (CSE) And Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML), Bringing Together Innovative Minds, Aspiring Engineers, And Technology Enthusiasts. It Provides A Dynamic Platform To Showcase Skills, Exchange Ideas, And Stay Updated With Emerging Technologies While Fostering Teamwork And Problem-Solving Abilities. The Event Features Coding Challenges, Paper Presentations, And Expert Talks Designed To Enhance Technical Skills And Inspire Innovation In The Field Of Computing.
