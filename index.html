About the Event

Our Technical Festival was a three-day event organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) of Mangayarkarasi College of Engineering. The festival was conducted to improve students’ technical skills, creativity, and confidence.

On Day 1, students participated in Paper Presentation (Xynapse), UI & UX Contest (Framux), Poster Presentation (AURA Post), and a Seminar (XPlore), which helped them share ideas and learn new concepts.

On Day 2 included exciting events like Coding Contest (Code Fest), Quiz (QuizTrix), and a Seminar (Brain X), where students tested their programming skills and general technical knowledge.

On Day 3, the festival concluded with a Hackathon (Hack Fest), where teams worked together to solve real-time problems. Overall, the technical festival was very useful and enjoyable, and it gave students a great opportunity to learn, explore, and develop their technical abilities.
